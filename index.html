A Research Lab
The core of the ESL where students, under the guidance of the resident study leaders and engineers, primarily conduct research related to modeling, control and automation of aerospace, terrestrial and underwater vehicles.
World-Class Academic Staff
The students would not be able to perform their research without the supervision of some world-class academic staff members who act as supervisors.
Commercial Work
The ESL is also involved in the development of advanced vehicle demonstrators for industry partners as well as satellite systems for commercial distribution.
Internships
Unlock your potential with occasional internships offered by the ESL for students from around the world (France, Germany, USA).
