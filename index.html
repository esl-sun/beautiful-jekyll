The Electronic Systems Laboratory is a postgraduate research laboratory focusing on autonomous vehicles, housed within the Electrical and Electronic Engineering Department of Stellenbosch University, South-Africa.

A Research Lab
The core of the ESL is graduate students, primarily conducting research related to control and automation of aerospace and terrestrial vehicles.
World-Class Academic Staff
The students would not be able to perform their research without the supervision of some world-class academic staff members who act as supervisors.
Commercial Work
The ESL is also involved in the development of advanced vehicle demonstrators and other projects for industry partners.
Internships
Unlock your potential with occasional internships offered by the ESL for students from around the world (France, Germany, USA).
